Mushy & Shroom

Lore

"We are one. We are Legion." Concealed in the depth of the Magic Forest, the mushroom army kept growing for countless years, until a mysterious threat loomed over the world. The mycelium's collective mind was then forced to reveal itself and join the Guardians of the Dominion, its truly newformed allies.

Mushy and Shroom are representatives of one big mushroom plant with a common mind, so although they are one Hero in the game, they define themselves as "we" and therefore we refer to them accordingly.
