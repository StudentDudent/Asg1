Recent Heroes Released In Mobile

Mushy & Shroom

"We are one. We are Legion." Concealed in the depth of the Magic Forest, the mushroom army kept growing for countless years, until a mysterious threat loomed over the world.
The mycelium's collective mind was then forced to reveal itself and join the Guardians of
the Dominion, its truly newformed allies.

Role: Mage
Main stat: Intelligence
Where to get? Special events
Faction: Way of Nature

Skills

Rank White: Perfect Copy
Conjures an inactive Shroom with a portion of its health missing. The copy has the hero's stats from the battle's start. It can be targeted by attacks, offensive spells, and healing spells. When the Shroom's health is fully replenished for the first time, it activates. After
that, it can attack and won't be able to replenish energy. You can summon no more than 5 Shrooms.

  • Starting health: 12323 (45% + 300) [At level 1]

Rank Green: Branshing Mycelium
Conjures mycelium with 3 inactive Spore Mushrooms with a portion of the health missing.
They can be targeted by attacks, offensive spells, and healing spells. Their max health is 30% of the hero's health at battle's start. Once the mushroom's health is fully replenished, it runs towards the enemies and explodes, dealing damage and spreading a Mushroom Spore cloud within a short range. The clouds
silences those caught within its range for 4.0 seconds.
Nearby enemies not affected by Mushroom Spores yet are prioritized when selecting the target.

  • Starting health: 2672 (10% Health) [At Level 1] Explosion damages: 2672 (10% Health) + 868.5 (25% Magic attack + 1404) [At Level 1]

Rank Blue: Rotting Seeds
Damages the nearest enemy. The damage is increased for each point of healing received by allies since this skill was last activated

  • Basic damage: 2328 (40% Magic attack + 1050) + 2672 (10% Health) [At Level 1]
  • Extra Damage: 60% of allied healing [At Level 1]

Rank Purple: Wild Growth
Inactive Shrooms and Spore Mushrooms can recover health by themselves. The effect stacks for each active Shroom on the Battle Field.

  • Inactive Shroom recover 267.17 (1% Health) + 537.76 (4% Magic Attack + 410) health per second [At Level 1]


Iris

Gifted and audacious in equal measure, Iris used to teach rookie sorcerers the art of magic, but her students' unequivocal adoration could never sate her ambitions. In her pursuit of
the arcane, she stole the ancient Umbra tome from the Archdemon's private library - only
to end up trapped within the book for years to come. Released at last and forever bound to the dark arts, Iris has emerged as a warlock!

Role: Mage
Main stat: Intelligence
Where to get? Special events
Faction: Way of Eternity

Skills

Rank White: Expose Soul
Iris exposes an unprotected part of the Soul in the nearest enemy's body for 13 seconds.
All damage taken by the Soul is transferred to its owner as pure damage.

  • Max health of the exposed Soul: 912,710 (200% Health + 350 * Level + 100)

Rank Green: Otherworldly Creature
Summons a familiar next to the farthest enemy on the battlefield. The familiar then
makes its way back to Iris, biting enemies along the way. Each successful bite deals more
damage than the last.

  • Damage for the first bite: 51,638 (37% Magic attack + 65 * Level)
  • Extra damage for each bite: 13,755 (9% Magic attack + 25 * Level)
  • Cooldown: 9 seconds
  • Initial cooldown: 4 seconds

Rank Blue: Demonic Synergy
Upon returning to Iris, the familiar grants her the Soul Fury charges accumulated by
successful attacks, boosting her next basic attack.

  • Extra damage for each charge: 43,703 (35% Magic attack + 20 * Level + 650)

Rank Purple: Seed of Destruction
Iris partly absorbs the health of all enemies on the battlefield and conjures a Seed of
Destruction. The Seed is then launched at the nearest enemy, dealing physical damage equal
to the total health absorbed.

  • Health absorbed from each enemy: 39,581 (25% Magic attack + 80 * Level + 3,200)
  • Cooldown: 14 seconds
  • Initial cooldown: 7 seconds


Mobile Only Character

Xe'Sha

Exiled to faraway worlds, she is overwhelmed by her doubts and knows well the bitterness
of loss. Her magic is born of pain, and as the pain grows on and on, so do her powers. How
long until she can no longer control them? No one knows.

Role: Mage, Support
Main stat: Intelligence
Where to get? Special events
Faction: Way of Chaos

Skills

Rank White: Deadly Ray
Xe'Sha drains 3 (3)% from the max health of any allies with over 25% of their health currently remaining, then attacks the enemy with the lowest magic defense. The damage dealt depends on the damage dealt to allies and Xe'Sha's magic attack. If the target is
killed as a result, allies recover double the health they lost. Xe'Sha's basic attacks deal magic damage and scale with Magic Attack.

  • Damage: 76,065 (125% Magic attack + 40 * Level) + 3% of allied max health

Rank Green: Spiritual Power
Xe'Sha fills her allies with power, damaging them by 3 (3)% of their max health if their
current health is above 25%. The empowered heroes gain a magic attack bonus for 4 seconds.
The bonus is doubled for Chaotic heroes

  • Magic attack bonus: 32,106 (50% Magic attack + 30 * Level)
  • Cooldown: 7 seconds

Rank Blue: Crimson Mist
Xe'Sha shrouds the battlefield in mist for 4 seconds, causing enemy basic attacks to miss.

  • Chance to affect an enemy is lowered if their level is above 120
  • Cooldown: 22 seconds
  • Initial cooldown: 4 seconds

Rank Purple: Soaring Might
Whenever an ally loses health from allied skills, Xe'Sha gains a permanent magic attack
bonus of 25 (25)% of the health lost by the ally.

  • Chance to trigger the skill is lowered if the ally's level is above 120
